Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound anchovies
  • 1 tablespoon minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans of tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. In a Dutch oven, cook sausage, anchovies, and onion over medium heat until well browned.
  2. Add garlic and crushed tomatoes. Cook, stirring occasionally, until liquid is thickened and sauce is hot
  3. Add tomato sauce and water. Stir well to combine.
  4. Add oregano, pepper, and crushed tomatoes. Stir well to combine.
  5. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  6. Place lasagna noodles in a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  7. Spread sauce on top of noodles.
  8. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
