Bacon Jam

Description

This is bacon jam

  • 1 pound bacon
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Set aside.
  3. Bake in preheated oven until golden brown, about 30 minutes.
